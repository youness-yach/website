# Discretionary FX — prop desk

<div class="deck">My Forex Fund · 2022–2023</div>

Before the models, the screen. Two years of discretionary price-action trading on a proprietary
FX desk, where the account was real and the drawdown was mine.

<div class="facts">
  <div><dt>Cumulative</dt><dd class="g">129%</dd></div>
  <div><dt>Trades</dt><dd>120+</dd></div>

The win rate is the interesting number, and I lead with it deliberately: **I lost more
trades than I won.** The return exists because risk-to-reward was held at 1:3 without
negotiation — no widening stops, no averaging down, no revenge sizing. That discipline is the
only thing that transferred cleanly into systematic work, and it's the reason I now trust
process over conviction.

Earned funded-trader status. MFF closed in 2023.
