# Semester at Sea — financial data pipeline

<div class="deck">ETL, SQL modelling, and revenue analysis</div>

An end-to-end pipeline over multi-year institutional revenue data: extraction, **schema
normalisation, validation**, and a SQL modelling layer that turned an inconsistent
historical record into something a finance team could actually make decisions from.

## What it produced

- Cross-fiscal revenue quantified and reconciled across years that didn't previously line up
- Bad-debt exposure modelled across student cohorts, isolating where the risk concentrated
- A repeatable pipeline, not a one-off spreadsheet — the analysis reruns when the data updates

Unglamorous plumbing, decision-grade output. Most of the value in analytics work sits in the
half nobody photographs.
