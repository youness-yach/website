# FX operations &amp; regulatory compliance

<span class="deck">Crédit International · 2024</span>

Worked on FX transaction workflows and **UEMOA regulatory compliance** inside West
African currency markets — a corner of FX with genuinely different mechanics from the majors:
a pegged regional currency, capital controls, and a compliance regime that shapes what can
actually trade.

It's the closest I've come to seeing how market structure gets *made* rather than
modelled. Useful grounding for anyone who otherwise only ever meets FX through a data feed.
