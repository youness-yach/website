# Visa (NYSE:V) — DCF &amp; comparables

<span class="deck">Equity research · published on Seeking Alpha</span>

A full valuation of Visa, built up from the economics of the payment network itself rather than
from a growth rate pasted onto last year's cash flow. Discounted cash flow, triangulated against
trading comparables, with **every assumption stated where a reader can attack it**.

## The approach

- Model the network: volume, take-rate, and the operating leverage that comes from both
- Build the DCF off those drivers, so the sensitivity table means something
- Sanity-check against comps — where the two disagree is where the thesis lives

Published publicly, which is the point. An unpublished valuation is a spreadsheet; a published
one is a position you have to defend.
